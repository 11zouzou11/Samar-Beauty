some facts about our treatments!

botox

Botox and its siblings in the botulinum family Vistabel, Azzalure and Dysport are proven drugs that can effectively reduce certain types of wrinkles - but not all. Because botox preparations work by limiting nerve impulses, only wrinkles caused by overactivity of the muscles under the skin can be treated. Wrinkles caused by e.g. skin laxity cannot be treated with botox.

fillers

A filler treatment is an injection treatment that is used to give volume, smooth out wrinkles, build up lost volume, lip augmentation and also rejuvenate the skin and treat scars. It gives an immediate result. Fillers consist of stabilized hyaluronic acid that is found naturally in the human body. Hyaluronic acid works in such a way that when injected, the old hyaluronic acid binds together and forms a more lasting result. The durability of the filler is individual and depends entirely on where it is injected and the patient's conditions. It is an extension work so the customer may need several treatments, especially the first year, until it gets the desired result.

plasma

A revolutionary electrostatic technology now makes it possible to tighten loose skin both on the face and on the body, without either a knife or injections! The treatment is completely superficial, ie. you never make holes or work inside the skin. As long as you follow the aftercare instructions correctly, there are no risks with the treatment.
